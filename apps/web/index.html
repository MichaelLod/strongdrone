Strongdrone

A competitive benchmark for LLM-piloted drones. Plug in any model from your byoky wallet and chase the high score.

  • Mission: the AI flies the quadcopter and dives onto enemy ground drones to score proximity strikes — every target down advances the run
  • Connect AI — any model in your byoky wallet works (Claude, GPT, Gemini, Grok, Llama, Mistral, …)
  • Pilot Mode — Round pauses physics while the LLM thinks (fair to reasoning models); Live never pauses (rewards low-latency tool-callers). Leaderboards are separated per mode.
  • Pick a scenario and hit Start Run. Personal best is tracked per (scenario × pilot mode).
  • Drag to orbit · scroll to zoom · R resets · Replay Last deterministically plays back any run
